## MCP penalty
Minimax Concave Penalty

$$
\begin{aligned} 
P_r(x;\lambda) = \begin{cases} \lambda \vert x \vert - \frac{x^2}{2r} \text{\quad if } \vert x \vert \leq r \lambda \\
\frac{r \lambda^2}{2} \end{cases}
\end{aligned}
$$
